Russia is more diverse than I thought

I always thought that Russia was just a bunch of East Slavic people with a whole lot of space, playing Counter Strike and sipping on Vodka. Obviously, most of Russia is empty and cold Siberia, but there are actually a heck lot of “countries” in Russia as well that took me by surprise. Just take a look at a map of all de jure republics within Russia

By Kwamikagami - Own work, CC BY-SA 4.0, Link

I think this is really interesting because each of these autonomous republics function as their own country. And it’s not like each Republic is the same, they all have their own unique quirks and characteristics.
